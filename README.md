## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a passionate **Embedded Systems Engineer** with **1 year** of hands-on experience building reliable, efficient, and innovative hardware-software solutions. I love bridging the gap between silicon and software — from writing bare-metal firmware to designing digital logic on FPGAs.

- 🔧 **Currently building:** Real-time embedded systems with STM32 & ARM Cortex
- ⚡ **Obsessed with:** Making microcontrollers do things they were never meant to do
- 🧠 **Always learning:** RTOS, FPGA design, and advanced digital systems
- 🤝 **Open to:** Collaborations on embedded, IoT, and hardware projects
- 🎯 **Fun fact:** I think in binary but dream in Python
